What is Hill Climbing?

Hill climbing is a mathematical optimization technique that belongs to the family of local search. Starting from an arbitrary solution, the algorithm attempts to find a better solution by incrementally changing a single element of the solution. It is called "hill climbing" because the process resembles climbing to the top of a hill, where each move takes you closer to the summit.

Climbing Tips

  • Always assess the gradient before stepping.
  • Use a mix of exploration and exploitation.
  • Avoid getting stuck in local maxima by restarting with different starting points.
